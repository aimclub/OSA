In the initial dataset, we had features with high percentages of missing
values, e.g., 34% for r1 relaxation, 28% for remanent magnetiza-
tion, and 21% for coercivity. To cope with that, we ﬁrst removed
the total of 211 samples such that each sample in the remaining
dataset had no more than 15% of missing values

we transformed highly correlated ge-
ometrical features into area to volume and max to min length
ratios reﬂecting size and shape anisotropy of nanoparticles

We also discarded nonin-
formative features of low variance, i.e., coercivity and remanent
magnetization values

+ After the ﬁnal dataset has been prepared, we divided it into

were predicted using 8 descriptors only

+ We normalized and log-scaled the data for each problem before splitting
to train and test sets in the 80/20 ratio
